# 🏏 IPL Auction Game

A real-time multiplayer IPL auction game built for friends who love cricket. Pick your team, bid on your favourite players, and build the best squad — just like the real IPL auction!

---

## 🎮 What Is This?

This is a **live, browser-based IPL auction** where one person hosts the auction and everyone else joins as a team. Players go up for bid one by one, teams place bids in real time, and whoever bids the highest wins the player. The goal? Build the strongest squad without blowing your entire budget.

Perfect for a group of cricket-obsessed friends who've always wanted to run their own IPL auction.

---

## 🚀 How To Play

### Step 1 — The Host (Auctioneer) Creates a Room
- Go to the link and click **Create Room**
- Enter your name and room name
- Optionally pick an IPL team for yourself if you want to bid too
- Share the **6-letter room code** with your friends

### Step 2 — Friends Join
- Everyone opens the same link on their phone or laptop
- Click **Join Room**, enter the room code
- Pick your IPL team and enter your name
- Wait in the lobby until everyone is ready

### Step 3 — Auction Starts
- The host clicks **Start Auction**
- Players come up one by one with a countdown timer
- Each team taps their bid button to place a bid
- Highest bid when the timer hits zero wins the player
- Rinse and repeat for all 342 players!

### Step 4 — End of Auction
- The host can end the game at any time using **End Auction**
- The game also ends automatically if all teams run out of budget
- Final standings show each team's full squad and spending

- ⏱ **Live countdown timer** — auto-extends when someone bids in the last few seconds

- 📊 **Live squad tracker** — see every team's budget, players bought and remaining funds

- 🛑 **End Auction button** — host can wrap up early with a confirmation step
- 💸 **Auto-end** — game ends automatically when all teams drop below ₹30L
- 📱 **Works on mobile** — join from your phone, no app download needed

## 🏆 Tips & Strategy

- **Don't blow your budget early** on marquee players — you still need depth
- **Watch other teams' budgets** in the left panel — if they're running low, you can outbid them easily
- **Jump-bid** above the minimum to scare off competition on players you really want
- **Overseas slots are limited** — max 8 per squad, so pick wisely
- **Unsold players** go back into the pool — the host can bring them up again
